How to Play

Spotly presents two nearly identical images. Your goal is to find and tap on the differences between them. Use hints if you get stuck!

Lives System

You start with 5 lives. Wrong guesses cost 1 life. Lives regenerate automatically (1 life per 30 minutes). Previously solved levels can be replayed for free.

Daily Spin

Spin the wheel once per day for free to win coins, extra lives, or special prizes. Additional spins can be purchased with coins.
