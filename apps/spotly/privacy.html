Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Data Collection

Spotly collects minimal data to provide you with a great gaming experience. Your game progress, coins, and lives are stored locally on your device.

Wallet Integration

When you connect your Solana wallet, we only access your public wallet address to sync rewards. We never have access to your private keys or funds.

Game Data

Your level progress, daily spin history, and achievements are stored locally. Solved levels can be replayed for free without using lives.

Usage Analytics

We use anonymous usage data to improve gameplay and fix bugs. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact the Spotly team.
