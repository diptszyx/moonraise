Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Information We Collect

Spinora collects minimal data to operate the game, including wallet public keys for rewards and leaderboard functionality. We do not key track of personal identifying information unless explicitly provided.

How We Use Your Data

We use your public key to distribute rewards and maintain your game profile. We analyze aggregated game data to improve game balance and user experience.

Blockchain Data

Please note that all transactions on the Solana blockchain are public and permanent. We cannot alter or remove data committed to the blockchain.

Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your game data. However, remember that you are responsible for the security of your own wallet private keys.

Contact Us

Questions about privacy? Reach out to the Spinora team.
