Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Neon X.O ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data when you play our game.

2. Blockchain Data

Neon X.O interacts with the Solana blockchain. When you connect your wallet:

  • We read your Public Address to identify you and display your balance.
  • Transaction history related to the game is public on the blockchain.
  • We NEVER have access to your private keys or seed phrases.

3. Gameplay Data

We collect temporary gameplay data (moves, match results, timestamps) to facilitate multiplayer matches and calculate rankings. This data is processed by our game servers.

4. No Personal Tracking

We do not collect names, emails, or phone numbers unless you voluntarily provide them for support. We do not sell your data to third parties.

5. Contact

For privacy concerns, please contact our support team.
